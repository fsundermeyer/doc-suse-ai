= Downloading AI models from {huggingface}

{huggingface} (https://huggingface.co/models) is an online repository for AI models.
It also supports models in the GGUF binary format optimized for quick loading and saving of models.
This procedure describes how to download and use these GGUF models from the {owui} interface. 

.Requirements
include::../snippets/openwebui-requirement-admin-privileges.adoc[leveloffset=1]

. Navigate your Web browser to https://huggingface.co/models and in the left
menu:Libraries[] section, click menu:GGUF[] to include only GGUF models to
choose from, for example, menu:gemma-7b[].
+
.Finding models on {huggingface} 
image::huggingface-selecting-model.png[Finding models on {huggingface},scaledwidth=75%]
+
. Click the model name to open its model card and select menu:Use this model[Ollama]
+
.{huggingface} model card
image::huggingface-mode-card.png[{huggingface} model card,scaledwidth=100%]
+
. From the menu:How to use[] pop-up, select the full path to the model, for example, `hf.co/google/gemma-7b`. 
+
.{ollama} download on {huggingface}
image::huggingface-ollama-download.png[{ollama} download on {huggingface},scaledwidth=50%]
+
. In the bottom left of the {owui} window, click your avatar icon to open the user menu and select menu:Admin Panel[] . 
. Click the menu:Settings[] tab and select menu:Connections[] from the left menu. 
. In the menu:Manage Ollama API Connections[] section, click the small "download" icon on the right to open the menu:Manage Ollama[] screen. 
+
.Downloading {ollama} models from {huggingface}
image::owui-dld-ollama-huggingface.png[Downloading {ollama} models from {huggingface},scaledwidth=100%]
+
. Paste the full path to the model tag from {huggingface} in the menu:Pull a model from Ollama.com[] input field and confirm by clicking the small download button on the right. 
+
.Entering model tag to download
image::owui-dld-ollama-huggingface-popup.png[Entering model tag to download,scaledwidth=75%]
+
The model download starts and, after it is finished, the model will be available as a base model for AI interaction.
