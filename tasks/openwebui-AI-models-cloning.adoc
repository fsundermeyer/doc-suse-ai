[[openwebui-ai-models-cloning]]
= Cloning existing AI models

ifndef::part-of-article[]
This procedure shows how to clone an existing custom AI model using {owui}.
endif::[]

.Requirements
* {empty}
+
include::../snippets/openwebui-requirement-admin-privileges.adoc[]

. In the {owui} interface, click menu:Workspace[] at the top of the left panel and select menu:Models[] . 
. In the list of AI models, hover the mouse over the model that you want to clone and click the three dots on its right. 
. From the contextual menu, select menu:Clone[] to open the same form as described in <<openwebui-ai-models-creating>> with all settings copied from the original model. The menu:Name[] field has `(Clone)` appended. 
. From the contextual menu, select menu:Clone[] to open the same form as described in with all settings copied from the original model. The menu:Name[] field has `(Clone)` appended. 
. Review the settings and create the cloned AI model with menu:Save & Create[].
