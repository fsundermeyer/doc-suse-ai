[[openwebui-ai-models-delete]]
= Deleting existing AI models

This procedure shows how to delete an existing custom AI model using {owui}. 

Using the {owui} interface, you can delete previously created AI models. 

.Requirements

include::../snippets/openwebui-requirement-admin-privileges.adoc[]
. In the {owui} interface, click menu:Workspace[] at the top of the left panel and select menu:Models[] . 
. In the list of AI models, hover the mouse over the model that you want to delete and click the three dots on its right. 
. From the contextual menu, select menu:Delete[] and then apply with menu:Confirm[] .
