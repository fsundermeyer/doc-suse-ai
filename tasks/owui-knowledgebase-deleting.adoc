[[owui-knowledgebase-deleting]]
= Deleting a knowledge base

This topic explains how to delete an existing knowledge base. 

.Requirements

include::../snippets/openwebui-requirement-admin-privileges.adoc[leveloffset=+1]
. In the {owui} interface, click menu:Workspace[] in the upper part of the left panel. 
. Select menu:Knowledge[] in the top menu and scroll to the knowledge base that you want to delete. Use the pager at the bottom if the knowledge base is not listed on the current screen. 
. In the knowledge base tile's top right corner, click the three dots and select menu:Delete[] . Confirm the deletion.
