[[openwebui-ai-models-creating]]
= Creating custom AI models

ifndef::part-of-article[]
This procedure shows how to create custom AI models using {owui}. 
endif::[]

.Requirements
* {empty}
+
include::../snippets/openwebui-requirement-admin-privileges.adoc[]

. In the {owui} interface, click menu:Workspace[] in the upper part of the left panel. 
. Select menu:Models[] in the top menu and click the small "plus" icon in the top-right corner of the screen. 
+
.Managing AI models
image::openwebui-workspace-models.png[Managing AI models,scaledwidth=50%]
+
. Click the default logo image in the top-left corner of the form to upload an avatar image for your model. 
. Enter the menu:Name[] of your new model and change the menu:Model ID[] if you are not happy with the provided one. 
. Specify the base AI model on which your model will be based on. 
. Enter a short description of your AI model. 
. Describe the model with custom tags so that other users can identify it easily. 
. To direct the AI model's responses, specify a menu:System Prompt[].
  Find more details about system prompts in {dsc}/suse-ai/1.0/html/AI-system-prompts/index.html. 
+
[TIP]
====
By clicking the small menu:Show[] option below the system prompt field, you can fine-tune the model's behavior by modifying its advanced settings. 
====
+
. Specify one or more menu:Prompt suggestions[]. They will be shown on the model's chat welcome screen. 
. To extend the model's knowledge base, select one or more documents or collections of documents from the preconfigured list. 
. In the menu:Tools[] , menu:Filters[] , menu:Actions[] and menu:Capabilities[] sections, activate the items that you want your model to use. 
+
[TIP]
====
To view the JSON representation of your model, click the small menu:Show[] option to the right of the menu:JSON Preview[] label. 
====
+
. Save your changes with the menu:Save & Create[] button.
