[[owui-prompts-deleting]]
= Deleting existing system prompts

ifndef::part-of-article[]
This procedure shows how to delete an existing system prompt. 
endif::[]

.Requirements
* {empty}
+
include::../snippets/openwebui-requirement-admin-privileges.adoc[1]
--
. In the {owui} interface, click menu:Workspace[] in the upper part of the left panel and select menu:Prompts[] . 
. Scroll to the prompt that you want to clone. Use the pager at the bottom if the prompt is not listed on the current screen. 
. Click the three dots to the right of the prompt that you want to clone and select menu:Delete[] . Confirm the deletion. 
--
