[[owui-prompts-modifying]]
= Modifying existing system prompts

This procedure shows how to modify an existing system prompt. 

.Requirements
** {empty}
+

include::../snippets/openwebui-requirement-admin-privileges.adoc[leveloffset=1]
. In the {owui} interface, click menu:Workspace[] in the upper part of the left panel and select menu:Prompts[] . 
. Scroll to the prompt that you want to modify. Use the pager at the bottom if the prompt is not listed on the current screen. 
. Click the corresponding pen icon on the right to open the form that enables you to edit the title and the description of the prompt. 
. Confirm with menu:Save {amp} Update[] .
