[[openwebui-ai-models-exporting-all]]
=== Exporting all existing AI models

This procedure shows how to export all existing AI models using {owui}. 

.Requirements

include::../snippets/openwebui-requirement-admin-privileges.adoc[leveloffset=+1]
. In the {owui} interface, click menu:Workspace[] at the top of the left panel and select menu:Models[] . 
. Click the menu:"Export Models"[] button at the bottom of the screen. 
. In the file selector, specify the path where you want to save the exported JSON file. Confirm with menu:Save[] .
