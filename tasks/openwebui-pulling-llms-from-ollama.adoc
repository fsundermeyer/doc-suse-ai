= Downloading AI models from {ollama}

{ollama} (https://ollama.com/) is an online repository that hosts open source AI models.
This procedure describes how to download and use these models from the {owui} interface. 

.Requirements
** {empty}
+

include::../snippets/openwebui-requirement-admin-privileges.adoc[leveloffset=1]
. In the bottom left of the {owui} window, click your avatar icon to open the user menu and select menu:Admin Panel[] . 
. Click the menu:Settings[] tab and select menu:Models[] from the left menu. 
. In the top-right corner of the screen, click the small "`download`" icon to open the menu:Manage Models[] screen. 
+

.Downloading {ollama} AI models [[_fig_owui_dld_ollama_model]]

image::owui-dld-ollama-model.png[Downloading {ollama} AI models,scaledwidth=100%]
. On the https://ollama.com/library[{ollama} library page], identify the model tag of an AI model that you want to download, for example, ``gemma3:4b``. 
. Paste the model tag in the menu:Pull a model from Ollama.com[] input field and confirm by clicking the small download button on the right. 
+

.Entering model tag to download [[_fig_owui_dld_model_tag]]

image::owui-dld-model-tag.png[Entering model tag to download,scaledwidth=75%]

+
The model download starts and, after it is finished, the model will be available as a base model for AI interaction.
