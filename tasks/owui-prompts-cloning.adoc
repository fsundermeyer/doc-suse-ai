[[owui-prompts-cloning]]
= Cloning existing system prompts

ifndef::part-of-article[]
This procedure shows how to clone an existing system prompt. 
endif::[]

.Requirements
* {empty}
+
include::../snippets/openwebui-requirement-admin-privileges.adoc[]
--
. In the {owui} interface, click menu:Workspace[] in the upper part of the left panel and select menu:Prompts[] . 
. Scroll to the prompt that you want to clone. Use the pager at the bottom if the prompt is not listed on the current screen. 
. Click the three dots to the right of the prompt that you want to clone and select menu:Clone[] . 
. Optionally update the title, the content and the activating command of the prompt. The command must be unique among all other existing system prompts. 
. Confirm with menu:Save & Create[] . 
--
