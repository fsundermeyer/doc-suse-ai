[[openwebui-ai-models-exporting]]
= Exporting existing AI models

ifndef::part-of-article[]
This procedure shows how to export an existing custom AI model using {owui}. 
endif::[]

.Requirements
* {empty}
+
include::../snippets/openwebui-requirement-admin-privileges.adoc[]

. In the {owui} interface, click menu:Workspace[] at the top of the left panel and select menu:Models[] . 
. In the list of AI models, hover the mouse over the model that you want to export and click the three dots on its right. 
. From the contextual menu, select menu:Export[] . 
. In the file selector, specify the path where you want to save the exported JSON file. Confirm with menu:Save[] .
