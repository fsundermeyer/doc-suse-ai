[[openwebui-user-revoking-access]]
=== Revoking access to {owui}

This topic explains how to revoke access to {owui} for an existing user account. 

.Requirements
** {empty}
+

include::../snippets/openwebui-requirement-admin-privileges.adoc[leveloffset=1]
. In the bottom left of the {owui} window, click your avatar icon to open the user menu and select menu:Admin Panel[] . 
. On the menu:Users[] screen, scroll to the user whose access to {owui} you want to revoke. Use the pager at the bottom if the user is not listed on the current screen. 
. {empty}
+
** To revoke the access while maintaining the user account, click the user's role button to the left of their name until it becomes "`pending.`"
** To delete the user account from the system, click the trash icon on the right and confirm the deletion.
