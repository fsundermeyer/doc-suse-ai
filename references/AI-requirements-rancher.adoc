= {ranchermanager} requirements

[[ai-reqs-rancher-hw]]
== Minimum hardware requirements

[role="tabs"]
Nodes for HA setup:: At least 3 nodes.
RAM:: A minimum of 32{nbsp}GB of RAM.
CPU:: At least 8 CPU cores.
Disk space:: At least 200{nbsp}GB of storage, preferably SSD.
